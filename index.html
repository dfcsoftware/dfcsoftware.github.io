Published: Mon 21 May 2525
By Don Cohoon

In linux.

tags: personal think

  • Introduction

  • Setup a New Server

Dedicated to my wife, Linh. She fixes the meals, I fix the house.

  • Following is a review of things to think about.

Early one foggy morning a white van whipped into the driveway and out pops two bearded fellows. "Hey, the wallpaper removers are here" I called out. Sure enough the camera alerted me as to an abnormal presence in the force.

It's not always been so easy, and assembling a security camera setup was something from Mad magazine in the 1960's that I achieved to some degree only lately. With an un-finished basement it was a breeze to run Cat-6 wires to each camera, even supplying power over ethernet (POE) to a latest breed of observers.

Following our birth to computers, from mechanical to electrical, monolithic to personal, microscopic chemicals and minerals beat their little binary hearts digested from human text. Standing on the hills of our ancestors we can only hope to provide something the world wants to use as long it works.

Thinking of such lofty goals, I am inspired to continue my journaley writings.

From here, for now;

-- Don
