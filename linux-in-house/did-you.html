Did You

Published: Fri 06 June 2025
By Don Cohoon

In writing.

Off to school,
then off to work,
now often late,
just another jerk.
On the string for independence.
Trick of the day.

*

Learn some more,
just try it.
Pour open the flow
let it fly,
compile and run,
check an alternate.

*

She's on the heal,
no place to go
no place to be,
it feels good to breathe.
Stay with me.

*

Would it be
a better place,
wait to see.
It feels good to breathe.
Stay with me.

*

Another day on the way,
nothing more to say
dream with me,
it feels good to breathe.
Did you?
Stay?

-- Don Cohoon; June-2025
