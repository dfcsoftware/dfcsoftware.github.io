Ordinary

Published: Thu 05 June 2025
By Don Cohoon

In writing.

I just wanted to be ordinary
another face in the crowd,
why can't I fit in.

He was extraordinary
and wanted a crowd,
fitting like a custom glove.

It was a long time brother
just staying alive,
shadows behind.

Was it so hard
to change a life,
over-night.

Respect the work
and it builds you,
as you build it.

Getting by for once
was your gift,
in my life.

Now I'm ordinary.
Isn't that extraordinary?
A new life.

--
