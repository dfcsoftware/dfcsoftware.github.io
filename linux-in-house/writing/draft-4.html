Boo Yaa

Published: 2025-07-19 10:20
Author: Don Cohoon

It’s the winter of my soul now when the steady march of lawn mowing eases out of the work week and occasional items like pruning enter.

Nightime doorbells ring in trick-or-treaters followed by gather planning. I searched for my coat and socks finding gloves thought to be lost.

Property tax was my reminder that the year is closing now so it’s time to enjoy the outside again.

A long motorcycle ride divided by lunch at German themed restaurant - Hollerbeck.

A walk to the neighborhood live band just sitting outside at night with friends.

It’s the winter of my soul now.

Regards

– Don Cohoon
