My Writing Post

Published: 2010-12-03 10:20
Author: Don Cohoon

Following is a story about my favorite ghost story.

It was bought at a local thrift shop sitting in the window too long; burnt, dry, yellow and tagged at 50 cents.

How could you go wrong?

Dive right in, the pages are fine.

Focus, emotion, dialog, characters and oh no, is there a plot?

Destruction of peace with chaos.

600 pages in one night, it spooks me as I continue.

Acid burns through darkness as lines of words stream in and out.

Visual movement overcomes silence with mind over matter.

Another book in the wall.

Solar man coaxes sleep….

– Don Cohoon
