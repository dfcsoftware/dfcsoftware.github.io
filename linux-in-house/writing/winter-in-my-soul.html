Winter in my Soul

Published: 2025-11-04 10:20
Author: Don Cohoon

It’s the winter of my soul now
when a steady march of lawn mowing
eases out of the work week
and occasional items like pruning enter.

Nightime doorbells ring in trick-or-treaters
followed by gather planning.
I searched for my coat and socks
finding gloves thought to be lost.

Property tax was my reminder that the
year is closing now so it’s time to enjoy the outside again.

Fortunately good friends are still around,
and I want be be a good friend too.

So, a long motorcycle ride divided by lunch
at German themed restaurant that rhymes with
Holler-Back was a great time that will be remembered.

Next, jamming on the beat-box
with two friends,
then a walk to a neighborhood live band,
finally,
just sitting outside at night,
chilling.

It’s the winter of my soul now.

Regards

– Don Cohoon
