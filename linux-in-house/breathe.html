Breathe

Published: Mon 14 July 2025
By Don Cohoon

In writing.

Are you receiving me?
Is this voice on?
Like writing on the moon
it's too small
to see.

Yet I write
and read
because that's what I do.
It is a desire
a wish
something I want to do.

We are all synchronized

Just as music is another
thing I do.
Input mostly now a-days
but there used to be a time
when I un-leashed hours of
fury on the drums.

Unforgettable times with other musicians
when that magic moment happens.
We are all synchronized
passing nods to handoffs
and crescendos through the bars
of invisible music sheets
we hardly read.

Yet we played on into the night
whether or not people listened,
because we could.

Whether reader or writer,
musician or listener,
breath in,
then out;
complete the circle.

-- Don Cohoon
