Following is a review of my favorite mechanical mouse.

The Ploopy!

It works great and feels good. Like a textured river rock, due to being printed on 3-D printers. In fact all the parts, except for the circuit board, are 3-D printed.

It is open source hardware, so anyone can print their own.

Only bad thing I found was it does not work with my MiniView KVM switch, :-(

– Don Cohoon

PS: In 2025 it is no longer shipped to US due to tariffs.
