Whos Pushing

Published: Mon 02 June 2025
By Don Cohoon

In writing.

Hey baby!
See me, feel me.

Hey pushing 10!
I got this.

Hey pushing 20!
Git out of the cave and find your own world.

Hey pushing 30!
Put me in coach, I have ambition and desire.

Hey pushing 40!
What is the place I find myself in?

Hey pushing 50!
Pack it in!

Hey pushing 60!
Save those nuts!

Hey pushing 70!
Un - ables? Just do it...

Hey pushing 80!
Give it back.

Hey pushing 90!
Smile...

-- Don Cohoon
