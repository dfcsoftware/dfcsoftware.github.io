Turn the Page

Published: Sat 07 June 2025
By Don Cohoon

In writing.

Oh no, you said it.

I shake it off,
and turn the page.

Another chapter in the day

something positive to say,

wipe those complaints away.

You did what?

Let's see who can whistle grass,
an age old elementary past
better than spitting forked seeds.

Another chapter in the day

something positive to say,

wipe those complaints away.

We do it all together.

One more spin around the neighborhood
with two wheels and a chain,
stop at the cold cow
to improve the brain.

Another chapter in the day

something positive to say,

wipe those complaints away.

Give your gift, some time.

Down to the river
talking to the wise ones
just be useful
give it a try.

Another chapter in the day

something positive to say,

wipe those complaints away.

--
